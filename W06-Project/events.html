Giving Back to Earth | Kampala

The vast majority of plastics are derived from fossil fuels (oil and natural gas). Extracting and refining these resources is an energy-intensive process that releases significant amounts of air pollutants, including greenhouse gases (like carbon dioxide and methane), volatile organic compounds (VOCs), and particulate matter. Reducing plastic production directly lowers the demand for these processes, thereby decreasing associated air pollution.
