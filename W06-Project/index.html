Eco-friendly tips

Get involved in community gardens, tree planting projects, or local environmental groups. These efforts expand the "green air spaces" in your community, leading to cleaner air and more natural environments for everyone..

Community gardening projects

Turn empty or underutilized land into vibrant green spaces filled with plants. This increases the overall amount of vegetation in an area.
