Pure Air Spaces

Description: A resource hub promoting clean air, greenery, and community well-being.

Site Name

The chosen site name is "Pure Air Spaces." This name emphasizes clean air, health, and eco-friendly living.

Site Purpose

Pure Air Spaces aims to provide practical resources, community features, and educational content to promote healthier living environments.

Scenarios

  • How can I reduce indoor dust effectively?
  • Where can I find community gardening projects near me?

Color Schema

  • #e9e9e9 : Used for borders.
  • #112a46, #000000, #134942, #ffffff: Used for text.
  • #ffffff, #f4f4f4, #134942: Used for backgrounds.

Typography

  • Roboto (Sans-serif): Used for headings.
  • Open Sans (Sans-serif): Used for body text.
